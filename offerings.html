Mana Movement Courses

Mana Movement is an Embodied Movement Method that is based on introducing the Feminine Pathways and Energetics of movement into the physical body as a way to balance Mind-Body-Spirit connection. It is an all-in-one Somatic Healing Technique that incorporates Mindfulness Meditation, Physical Activation, and Spiritual Liberation. It is accessible to all bodies, and there is no former experience necessary.

Programs do not have to be completed in order. You are free to choose whichever one calls to you!
