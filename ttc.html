Mana Movement is an “all-in-one” embodied movement method developed by Mana Mei. This simple yet profound practice flows through a series of guided movement sequences, focusing on spinal mobility, whole body fluidity, release, breath-body connection, and returning to the Feminine Pathways.

Through cultivating sustained focus and presence with the feeling body, Mana Movement encourages harmonious reconnection to your physical body, awakens creative life force energy, and opens the pathway to somatic healing through the holistic trifecta of mindfulness meditation, physical activation, and spirit awakening.

Who is this training for?

This training is for women who have the desire to teach the Mana Movement Practice using the specific structure of the practice as designed by Mana Mei. This practice follows a series of specific, and carefully designed movement patterns and sequences that will create a powerful and successful experience of self-connection and physical and energetic activation through the entire body.

Through successfully completing this training, you will have the experience, knowledge, and permission to teach Mana Movement online and in-person using the Mana Movement brand. This is the ONLY current training which allows you to use the Mana Movement name and specific structure of the practice.

Training Prerequisites

  • Minimum 3-months of active and regular personal Mana Movement Practice
  • Personal experience with self-development, personal transformation, or embodiment work
  • Have completed at least TWO of the following offerings by Mana Mei:
    • Mana Movement Foundations Course
    • Mana Movement Full Course
    • Mana Movement Maternity Course
    • confiDance Course
    • All YouTube Mana Movement Practices

OR the Liberation Through Movement Mentorship Program

Training Structure

The Mana Movement Teacher Training is a 16-Week Live Online Program where you will be held in a container of support and accountability. You will have lifetime access to all training content and weekly live interaction with Mana Mei and other students in the training.

This training consists of…

  • Extensive PDF Training Manual with Foundational Theory and detailed breakdown of all movement sequences
  • Lifetime access to all pre-recorded demo videos of all movements and sequences, Plus lifetime access to additional supplementary embodied movement meditations and practices (for personal use)
  • Weekly Live Group Transmissions with Mana Mei, covering in-depth conceptual theory of Mana Movement and Feminine Embodiment, Somatic Healing, Nervous System Regulation, The Art of Authentic Facilitation, Building a business the Feminine Way, Creating Trust & Authority as a leader, and much more…
  • Bi-weekly (every other week) Small Group Practice Teaching Pod calls lead by assigned Assistant Teacher who is a Certified Mana Movement Facilitator
  • 1-on-1 and small group support from Certified Mana Movement Teachers
  • Bi-weekly (every other week) Written and Video Homework Assignments
  • Exclusive TELEGRAM Group with all students and Assistant Teachers for Support, connection, inspiration, and accountability
  • Lifetime access to all training curriculum and Transmission recordings
  • This training is open to women all over the world! We are a global community and we do our best to accommodate all time zones in the scheduling of the training. No matter where you live, we can make this training work for you.
  • This training is designed with busy women in mind! Hey, we all have a lot going on in our lives… children and families, jobs and businesses, travel and self-care. This training has been thoughtfully crafted to fit into your life and everything else you have going on through a flexible “self study” aspect, plus availability of call replays and intentional “catch-up” integration weeks.
  • This training container will ensure you never feel alone along the journey! Through carefully designed rings of support, ranging from 1-on-1 to whole group connection, you will never feel alone in your training experience and you will always have someone to turn to whether it be for support, inspiration, accountability, or authentic connection!

As a certified Mana Movement Facilitator...

You can teach in-person and virtual Mana Movement classes to a variety of audiences and client demographics, in English OR translated into other languages.

You can integrate the Mana Movement Practice into 1-on-1 work with clients if you are a coach, therapist, yoga teacher, or space holder of any other modality You have permission to use the official Mana Movement logo and branding to promote your classes and gain students, supported by the increasing popularity of the Mana Movement practice world-wide Mana Movement is a perfect class to offer at spaces dedicated to movement, embodiment, dance, or meditation. It is a versatile offering that can supplement a variety of other practices and modalities of healing including yoga, ecstatic dance, breathwork, tantra, mindfulness meditation, other dance techniques, women’s circles, cacao ceremonies, and so much more!
